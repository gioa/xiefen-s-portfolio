Opera Mini Chinese version 7.8.2 Shipment

Project Background:
Opera Mini is a lightweight mobile browser with 250 million monthly active user. Its Chinese version is derived from the international one and has been adapted to meet local needs and stay within Chinese government Great Firewall.


Role: Lead Product Manager

Teams: Product managers, Operation team, Engineers, Designers, Marketing team

Duration: 2 months


Overview:

I was assigned as the product owner of Opera Mini and managed to ship version 7.8.2 with key updates and within deadline.

As the lead product manager, I planed for the next product iteration and discussed the potential shippable features with other product managers.
Then I prioritized them and added to the product backlog, with which I reported to the executive team and explained the value delivered.
After finalizing the features, I drove the overall product development by coordinating tasks and resources within multi-functional teams.
At last I worked with marketing team to ship the new version. Sometimes the shipment followed an A/B testing strategy and based on the feedback we reworked to ensure a high quality delivery.
