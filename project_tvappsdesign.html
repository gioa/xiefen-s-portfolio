Design of TV App

Project Background:
The Opera TV Store is an HTML5-based storefront displaying web applications made for TV(called as TV Apps) and run from the cloud. It gives the user a comfortable, “lean-back” experience, using a standard TV remote control to navigate, select and launch apps easily. Like other stores, it requires various TV apps spanning from video (the most popular category), music, social networking, games, news and information.


Role: QA Engineer

Teams: External developers and designers

Duration: 3 months


Overview:

My contributions are mainly focused on leading the outsourcing project of developing 10+ Chinese TV apps for Opera TV Store.

As the QA engineer in the TV team, I was responsible for moderating and publishing TV apps submitted by external developers or content partners. During this period I acquired TV apps design principles and understood potential developing issues. So when Opera TV Store planed to land on China TV Market and required various local TV apps, I took charge of supporting our local content partners to build TV apps and also led an outsourcing team to develop TV apps for content partners who did not have developer resource.


I also gave speech as guest speak on design of TV apps at Html5 Top conference 2012, to motivate HTML5 developers to build TV apps for Opera TV Store.
